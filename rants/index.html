  • The number of Israelis killed in road accidents since independence in 1948 is greater than the number killed in wars and terrorist attacks. In 2002, 522 Israelis were killed in road accidents. 20,500 Israelis have been killed in wars and terrorist attacks between May 1948 and May 2003, compared with 21,600 killed in road accidents. (source) So why do good numerate people worry more about my visiting Israel for the terror than for the autos? (Aside: the number of death from cars in Israel is about 1/2 that for a comparable number of Americans.)
