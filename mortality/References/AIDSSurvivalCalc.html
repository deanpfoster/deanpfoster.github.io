Calculations for AIDS Survival Rate

  1. The data in the paper states that the mean survival period of a person diagnosed with AIDS is 2.25 years, and that 97% of those diagnosed with AIDS actually died of AIDS.
  2. Thus, the lambda value for AIDS for a person diagnosed for AIDS would be 0.97/2.25 to get 0.4311111.
  3. This lambda value replaces the original lambda for AIDS for all ages.
