Calculations for Cirrhosis of the Liver, Alcohol-Related Cancers, Heart Diseases and Stroke due to Alcohol Consumption

  1. The data in the paper gives un-normalized relative risks for Cirrhosis of the Liver, Alcohol-Related Cancers, Heart Diseases and Stroke segregated by sex and amount of alcohol consumed per day.
  2. The risks are then normalized.
  3. The lambda values for Cirrhosis of the Liver, Alcohol-Related Cancers, Heart Diseases and Stroke are changed accordingly.
