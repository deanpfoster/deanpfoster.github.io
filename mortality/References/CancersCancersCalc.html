Calculations for Breast, Ovary, Colorectal, Stomach and Lung Cancer due to Family History of Breast, Colorectal, Stomach and Lung Cancer

  1. The data in the paper gives un-normalized relative risks for breast, ovary, colorectal, stomach and lung cancer for women segregated by family history of breast, colorectal, stomach and lung cancer. No data for men were available.
  2. The risks are then normalized.
  3. The lambda values for the various cancers are changed accordingly.
