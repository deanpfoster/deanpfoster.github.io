How Long Will you Live?

by Dean P. Foster, Choong Tze Chua, and Lyle H. Ungar.

The web provides a wonderful way of presenting information about health habits. By allowing the you to enter health information, a calculation can be made to predict how long you will live.

We developed such a life calculator (long version). The predictions are based on data about Americans so the forecasts won't be as useful for people from different cultures.

The following will all make predictions as to how long you will live:

  • Our short form life calculator (less than one minute)
  • Our longer version of the life calculator (about 5 minutes)
  • Several calculators which are targeted at the elderly.
  • NW Mutual's The longevity game
