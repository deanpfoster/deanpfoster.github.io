Second homework for Stat 112

Do this assignment on your own.

  • Find an article (either from something like the New York Times, or from a journal in your area of study) which discusses either a simple regression or a multiple regression.
  • Read it critically from a statisitical point of view.
  • Write a one page comment on the paper. Did they do the regression correctly? What you you like to see done differently? Did they make any errors? did they prove their statistical story they were telling?
