  • Randomized response, a justification for obtuse questions.
  • Have you used drugs (i.e. committed a felony) or were you born in a month which doesn't have the letter "r" in it?
  • Hint: May, June, July
  • Suppose 1/2 of the people say yes. Then: 1/3 said yes because of birthday. 1/6 said yes because of felony. 1/2 said no because of wrong birthday AND no felony.
  • We are only interested in those NOT born in May, June or July
  • Conclusions: 25% have committed a felony.
  • Better still would be to have them roll a die
