  • Histogram = summary of data with a graph

  • Statistics = summary of data with a number

  • Examples of statistics

    • mean = average
    • variance = average squared deviation
    • standard deviation = squareroot of variance
    • median = middle of data
    • Range = top - bottom (a bad statistic)
    • IQR = top 25% - bottom 25%

  • The key advantage of a statistic is that you can present many of them all at once

    (Think about drawing 10 histograms. It generates a very cluttered picture.)
