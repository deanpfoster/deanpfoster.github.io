The expected value shouldn't change. On average, the company should still win 30 of all its bids, and 30 of 3 is 0.9, as we found in the the previous exercise. What should change is the variance. The statement of the exercise indicates that there's higher probability that the company will either win all its bids or lose all its bids, that is, that Y will come out either 3 or 0.
