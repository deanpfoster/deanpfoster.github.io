JMP Sandwich Estimator

To generate a sandwich estimate of a SE in JMP do the following:
  1. Run the multiple regression like usual
  2. Save the leverage plots
    • this generates 2 columns for each of your leverage plots
    • One column is the Y of the plot the other is the X of the plot
    • Check this by doing a simple regression.
  3. Center the X and Y for the variable you want to estimate
    • You will have to create new columns
    • Just subtract off the mean
    • Now you can compute the beta-hat by averaging the product of these two columns divided by the average of the square of the X column. Check this by creating a product column and the square column averaging and dividing.
  4. Compute the variance by sum(LX2 LY2)/sum(LX2)
    • Use the product column you create above, call it P.
    • sum(p2) is simple the mean squared plus the variance of P.
    • A simillar argument works for LX.
    • Alternatevly you can use a formula: sum(p2)sum(LX2)
