Supply and demand estimation

  • Difficulties in observational studies

    • Suppose you watch the price go up and consumption goes down, why might this occur? (answer: downward sloping demand curve)
    • Suppose you watch the price go up and production goes up, why might this occur? (answer: upward sloping supply curve)
    • NOTE: production = consumption.
    • Suppose you watch the price go up and production/consumption stays the same? (answer: ???)
    • Draw two sets of (P,Q) graphs that have same observations, but different elasticities
