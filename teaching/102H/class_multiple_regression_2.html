t statistics

  • |t| > 2 is .05 level of significance
  • Tests if the slope MIGHT be zero.
  • If the slope were actually zero, then less than 5% of the time should the |t| be bigger than 2.
  • JMP gives p-values that are easier to interpret. Is the p-value less than .05?
